Registration

Author Registration:

  • Full Conference: $300
  • Workshop: $200

Participant Registration:

  • Full Conference: $150 Complimentary

Rules:

  • Author registration deadline for full conference and workshop is August 13, 2021.
  • Each paper must have an author registration in the corresponding category, and one author registration can cover one paper only. A workshop paper author can pay the full conference rate to cover a workshop paper and attend both the main conference and workshops. 
  • Any full conference rate covers both the main conference and workshops.
  • To guarantee publication of a WiOpt (conference and workshop) paper, at least one author should present the work at the conference, which is a virtual event this year. We are going to announce more details of the virtual event soon, including information and instructions on how to prepare for a virtual presentation.
  • Each paper needs an author registration in the corresponding category, and one author registration can cover one paper.  A workshop paper author can pay the full conference rate to cover a workshop paper and attend both conference and workshops. 

Instructions for Registration:

  • On the registration site, before proceeding with the registration, you are required to create a personal account if you have not registered with WiOpt 2021 before. After filling in your information at the bottom-right of the page, click “Register” to continue. Meanwhile, the system will send an email to ask you to set the password. Please make sure the email address “noncredit@temple.edu” is not banned and search the email with the title “Temple University New Account”.
  • Next, the registration site will ask you to choose your registration package (Author, Participant, or Student). After choosing one of the packages, click “Continue”.
  • Fill in more detailed personal information and choose the payment method on the next page, click “Pay and Register”.
  • You will receive a confirmation email and a receipt email separately. After registration, you can use your user name and password on the registration site to log in as an existing user. Once logged in, you can check your current registration information.

Instructions for Participants:

  • Sign up: If you have not yet done so, visit the online conference website ( https://wiopt.online ) and sign up for a new account with your registration ID (i.e., the Transaction Basket number) that was provided to you when you registered for the conference. If you have already signed up for an account in WiOpt 2020, you can continue to use that account, but you will need to enter your new registration ID from WiOpt 2021.
  • Note that it could take a few days for the Registration Chair to import the registration information into the online conference website. If you have registered and your registration ID (i.e., the Transaction Basket number) is not yet activated in the system after a few days, send an email message to the Registration Chair, Yan Wang < y.wang@temple.edu >, with your proof of registration.
  • Activate your account: Follow the link in the email message you receive after signing up to activate your account. Alternatively, visit your profile and click the “Activate this account” button.
  • To enter the Zoom meeting for any of the sessions you plan to attend, go to the day and the session on the online conference website, and click the “Enter Zoom” button. The button will become active a few days before the conference.
  • At any time during the session, you may type your questions in the chat window in the Zoom meeting. After the talk concludes, the session chair will be selecting the questions to be answered by the speaker of the talk.
  • At the end of each talk in the session, there will be a Q & A period, when you can feel free to ask questions, either by typing them in the chat window, or by unmuting your microphone and speaking directly.
